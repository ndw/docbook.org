DocBook Publishers: The Definitive Guide

DocBook Publishers: The Definitive Guide is a special edition of DocBook: The Definitive Guide designed especially to cover the Publishers Schema.

This is an early, experimental version. The elements and content models are correct, the descriptions are (mostly) correct. But the prose is still quite incomplete. Also, there are known formatting and linking problems.

Versions available:

  • Version 1.0.0 is a beta version of Publishers 1.0 (based on DocBook V5.0).

  • Version 1.1.0 is an even more experimental beta version of Publishers 1.1b1 (based on DocBook V5.1b3).
